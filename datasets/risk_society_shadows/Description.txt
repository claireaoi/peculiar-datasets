risk society shadows

Here is a dataset exposing the shadows of the security economy: shadows of cameras, barriers, grids, lockers... and other bits of the surveillance or risk prevention apparatus.

Nota Bene: A risk society is somewhere between "a society increasingly preoccupied with the future (and also with safety), which generates the notion of risk," [Anthony Giddens] and "a systematic way of dealing with hazards and insecurities induced and introduced by modernisation itself" [Ulrich Beck].
